Ushio Noa

Noa is the secretary of the student council "Seminar" at Millenium Science School.

She has a very good memory and can memorize almost everything she sees or hears once, including decisions made at the "Seminar" and records of the contents of meeting.

Nekozuka Hibiki

A member of the Engineering Dept. at Millenium School. Despite being unsociable and unsophicated in speech, she exhibits a prominent natural talent in engineering and has invented many strange marvelous objects.

Her inventions are practically flawless, and while it wouldn't be too much to call all of them masterpieces, there's always at least one bizzare feature included that leaves their users bewildered.

Omagari Hare

The Engineer for a group hackers "Veritas" at Millenium Science School. She's friendly to the other students and a good listener.

She is one of the brightnest minds at Millenium, and most of Millenium's cutting-edge devices have been developed by her. However, despite her ability and achievement, she doesn't go bragging about it.
